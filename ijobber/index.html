About iJobber

iJobber is a powerful Chrome extension that transforms your job search by generating personalized, professional cover letters and CVs tailored to specific job postings. Using advanced AI technology, iJobber analyzes job requirements and creates customized application documents in seconds.

How It Works

  1. One-Time Setup: Upload your CV or connect your LinkedIn profile
  2. Choose AI Provider: Select from 6 AI providers (including a free option)
  3. Browse Jobs: Navigate to any job posting on LinkedIn, Indeed, or Seek
  4. Generate Documents: Click the extension icon to create tailored documents
  5. Download & Apply: Get your documents in PDF, HTML, or Markdown format

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

iJobber ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how iJobber handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

iJobber does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to our servers.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • CV or resume content
  • LinkedIn profile data
  • Job posting information
  • Generated cover letters or CVs
  • API keys or credentials
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Usage analytics or statistics

3. How Your Data is Handled

Local Storage Only:

  • Your CV, LinkedIn profile data, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's storage API
  • This data never leaves your device except when sent to your chosen AI provider (see below)
  • You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the extension

AI Provider Communication:

  • When you generate a document, your CV data and job posting information are sent to your chosen AI provider's API
  • This communication is direct between your browser and the AI provider
  • We do not intercept, store, or have access to this data
  • Each AI provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data

4. Third-Party AI Providers

iJobber supports the following AI providers. When you use their services, their respective privacy policies apply:

  • Puter.com: https://puter.com/privacy
  • Azure OpenAI: https://privacy.microsoft.com/
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy/
  • Anthropic Claude: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AWS Bedrock: https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/

5. Permissions Explained

activeTab: Allows reading job posting details from the current tab when you click the extension icon.

scripting: Enables extraction of job information from LinkedIn, Indeed, and Seek pages.

storage: Stores your CV, settings, and API keys locally in your browser.

downloads: Allows you to download generated documents to your computer.

Host Permissions: Access to LinkedIn, Indeed, and Seek domains to read publicly visible job postings.

6. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API
  • API keys are stored in encrypted Chrome storage
  • No data is transmitted to iJobber's servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Communication with AI providers uses HTTPS encryption

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View stored data through the extension settings
  • Delete your CV or LinkedIn profile data at any time
  • Change or remove API keys
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

iJobber is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Open Source

iJobber's code is transparent and can be reviewed. We encourage security researchers and users to examine our code to verify our privacy claims.

In Plain English:

iJobber doesn't collect your data. Everything stays on your computer. When you generate a document, your information goes directly from your browser to the AI provider you chose. We never see it, store it, or have access to it. You're in complete control.
